Kiran Mazumbar-Shaw-Biotech queen of India

– Biocon From Garage Startup to Healthcare Pioneer

Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw’s journey exemplifies Indian entrepreneurs success stories, marked by perseverance and innovation in the biotechnology sector.

Remarkable journey of Queen of biotech:

  • This woman started her company in a Small garage with just Rs.10,000
  • Kiran was born in Bengakuru to Gujarati parents
  • She wanted to go to Medical school but couldn't get a scholarship
  • Her father suggested her to study fermentation science and become a brewmaster and with her own interest she pursued it.
  • She went to Melbourne University in Australia
  • She was the only woman in the class and stood first in everything.
  • When seeking work in India ,she was told she couldn't be hired as a master brewer because "It's a man's job"
  • She found a job in Scotland and met the founder of biocon there.
  • She grabbed the opportunity and returned to India to setup it's Subsidiary
  • Kiran started with just Rs.10,000 in small garage
  • Soon,Biocon became the first Indian company to manufacture and export enzymes
  • Kiran Mazumbar is now a billionare with networth of $2.5 billion(Rs.20,963 Cr)
  • Kiran Mazumdar’s story serves as a testament to the power of determination, resilience, and seizing opportunities against all odds.
  • Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw is a first-generation entrepreneur and global business leader with over 4 decades of experience in biotechnology.
  • Initially, she faced many credibility challenges because of her youth, gender and her untested business model.
  • She was unable to secure funding for her company in the early stages of her business. A chance meeting with a banker at a social event finally enabled her to get her first financial backing.
  • Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw's role model was her father who inspired her to strive for excellence and be second to none.
  • She says that :'Failure is temporary. Giving up is permanent'.' Innovation and commerce are as powerful tools for creating social progress as they are for driving technological advancement.
  • Kiran Mazumdar-Shaw demonstrates many important leadership traits, including a dedication to diversity and inclusion, great vision and strategic thinking, excellent communication, and teamwork.

-“I believe that entrepreneurship is about being able to face failure, manage failure and succeed after failing.”

---Kiran Mazumbar
